About Us

We believe fashion is for everyone. And don’t let your wallet tell you any different. Founded in 2017, Alaya by Stage3 was conceptualized to bring a simple vision to life; make runway-ready styles available to you at the click of a button.
With a finger on the pulse of the latest trends, we asked your favourite celebrity stylists, bloggers, and influencers, to handpick and curate timeless classics for that envious Instagram feed. Dreading the wedding? Stay-cation on the cards? Or is it just the mid-week blues? Look no further. With a wide selection of ethnic and contemporary classics, consider this your one-stop shop for all occasions.
We like to keep it: Elegant, Effortless and Edgy. And did we mention, Easy! Give your sedentary staples a prim revival and have them delivered straight to your doorstep. Didn’t you hear, convenient is the new chic?
